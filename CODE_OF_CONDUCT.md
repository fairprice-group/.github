# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

FairPrice Group firmly believes in maintaining the highest standards in business
practices to promote public confidence and trust without compromising integrity.
This Code of Conduct provides guidance on what we believe is important, the way
we are expected to behave and make ethical decisions every day in the course of
our work.

This Code applies to everyone contributing to or participating in FairPrice
Group projects, regardless of position.

## Ethical Standards

All contributors and participants must:

- Diligently perform all duties in good faith, with the highest standards of
  honesty, integrity, responsibility and professionalism
- Comply with all applicable laws, policies, regulations, standards and
  procedures
- Treat everyone with respect and courtesy
- Act in the best interests of the Group
- Act with integrity in all dealings and do the right thing even when no one is
  watching
- Uphold the reputation of the Group and refrain from any actions that could be
  detrimental

## Diversity, Equity and Inclusion

We prioritise diversity, equity and inclusion in all aspects of our operations.
Every individual, regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation,
ability, religion, or background, deserves to be treated with respect, fairness
and dignity.

Our commitment is to build and foster a diverse environment based on trust and
equal opportunity, where every contributor feels valued, heard and included. We
recognise that embracing diversity strengthens our team, enhances creativity and
drives innovation.

**Discrimination in any form is prohibited.** As a multicultural and multiethnic
community, it is crucial to work together harmoniously, demonstrating sensitivity
to colleagues with diverse ethnicities, religions and beliefs to foster a safe
and respectful environment.

We must also avoid behaviours such as insults, bullying, disparagement,
intimidation, threats, violence, or harassment. Harassment is determined by its
impact on others, encompassing actions like using threatening language,
unwelcome conduct, unlawful stalking, or engaging in doxing.

## Safe and Secure Environment

We prioritise the well-being of all participants. This includes promoting a
culture of accountability. We must promote a safe and healthy working
environment by following established protocols and avoiding actions that may
cause harm to ourselves or others.

## Confidentiality

Information about the Group is a valuable asset. Confidential information --
including trade secrets, business plans, customer data, financial information
and proprietary systems -- must be protected and must not be:

- Published or disclosed without authorisation
- Used for personal benefit
- Copied or reproduced without authorisation
- Made accessible to unauthorised persons

## Intellectual Property

All intellectual property and works created in connection with FairPrice Group
projects belong to the relevant FairPrice Group entity. Unless otherwise
authorised, such works must not be disclosed, published or used for personal
purposes.

## Fraud and Corruption

We uphold a zero-tolerance policy against fraud and corruption. Any such
situation must be reported promptly.

## Reporting and Enforcement

### Reporting Concerns

If you encounter violations of this Code, unethical behaviour, or have
concerns, you may report through:

- **[FPG Whistleblowing Portal](https://www.fairpricegroup.com.sg/whistleblowing/):**
  Submit reports via the online form or voice recording. Anonymous reports are
  accepted. All reports are treated confidentially on a need-to-know basis, and
  informants are protected against reprisals for reports made in good faith.

**Do NOT open a public GitHub issue for sensitive conduct matters.**

### Response

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All
project maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the
reporter.

### Consequences

Non-compliance with this Code may result in corrective action, including but
not limited to:

- A warning (written or verbal)
- Temporary or permanent restriction from participation
- Removal from the project

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all FairPrice Group project spaces,
repositories and community interactions. It also applies when an individual is
officially representing the organisation in public spaces.
